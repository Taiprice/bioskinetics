Our Founder

Products around the world are designed for manufacturing convenience, cheap processing, and 'average' skin. That's just it — you don't have 'average' skin. You have your skin.

Tai Price, the founder and owner of Bioskinetics, is not without his own set of obstacles. From an early age, he struggled with Eczema, sensitive skin, and allergies.

"I've spent years complaining about the lack of products that fit my guidelines. If it doesn't have one of my allergies, it has the other. If it doesn't have that allergy, it has another. And unfortunately, when I find a product that is safe for me, it comes in bland, boring, containers that don't feel me."

"You know that feeling when you walk into the personal care section and see the hundreds of colorful bottles that contain all of your allergies so you have to settle for the same boring white bottle every time? Products for curly hair, thin hair, thick hair, wavy hair, frizzy hair, thinning hair, etc. It really makes you realize how left out of the system you are."

"So I created this company for people to realize that they matter. Just because your skin is a little bit more complicated, doesn't mean you don't deserve the same care and effort put into your products as everyone else. So instead of waiting for someone to make an accessible system, I created it myself."

Bioskinetics is a company built on customizable cosmetic line-ups — allowing for self expression and, most importantly, allergy-safe products.

Our Story

Beauty that's built for everyone.

At Bioskinetics, we believe that beauty should be accessible to everyone. This belief sparked our journey and continues to guide everything we do today. We saw a need for products that not only enhance natural beauty but also make people feel confident in their own skin, without barriers or limitations. Our goal has always been to create a brand that supports self-expression and confidence, offering cosmetics that help you show up as your best self every day.

Our Ingredients

Formulated with intention.

At our company, we take pride in offering high-quality, thoughtfully selected ingredients that prioritize both performance and safety. All of our formulations are fully customizable, allowing you to tailor each product to your unique needs and preferences. We understand how important it is to avoid allergens, which is why any ingredient in our products can be easily swapped for a safe alternative.
